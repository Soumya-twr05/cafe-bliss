About Cafe Bliss

Founded in 2023, Cafe Bliss was created to be a warm corner for friends, students, and creators. Our coffee beans are sourced ethically, and our snacks are baked fresh daily. The interiors mix wood and soft lighting to create a relaxing ambiance perfect for studying or catching up with friends.

  • Locally sourced ingredients
  • Cozy workspace with charging points
  • Seasonal specials every month
